    Unlike other actors, Tom Cruise doesn’t make an effort to keep his personal life out of the spotlight, from his relationship with Nicole Kidman to his public divorce from Katie Holmes. The actor also likes to showcase his relationship with his children from time to time. For instance, Steven Spielberg believes that Cruise is a great father, and he would often stop filming simply to answer his kids’ phone calls.

    Cruise is rather close to his and Nicole Kidman’s shared children, Isabella Jane and Connor Cruise. But how close is the actor to Suri Cruise, his shared daughter with Katie Holmes? Well, over the years, the media has speculated what Cruise’s relationship with Suri is like. However, Holmes has called them out for their ‘inaccuracies’ on more than one occasion and has instead revealed what her daughter’s relationship with the actor is really like.

    Related: Tom Cruise And Katie Holmes Allowed Suri To Drink Starbucks Coffee As A 4-Year-Old

    Tom Cruise And Katie Holmes' Relationship Timeline

    Tom Cruise and Katie Holmes were first linked together in 2005 when the pair looked rather cozy on a trip to Europe together. That same year, Cruise went wild on an interview with Oprah and, while jumping up and down, discussed his relationship with the Dawson’s Creek star for the first time. "I'm in love. I'm in love and it's one of those things where you want to be cool, like, ‘Yeah I like her,'" he told the TV host. "That's not how I feel. I wanted to meet this person and I met her and she's extraordinary."

    Cruise and Holmes got engaged in 2005 after only a couple of months of dating. A year later, the two welcomed their first and only daughter together, Suri Cruise. In 2006, the pair finally tied the knot with a lavish wedding at the Odescalchi Castle in Italy. In 2010, Holmes told Marie Claire that her marriage with Cruise was "inspiring and fun." Adding that, "It's an amazing life, but we always remind ourselves of how incredibly lucky we are."

    Related: Tom Cruise Set The Record Straight On If He Banned Katie Holmes From Taking Drugs During Her Pregnancy Delivery

    While their marriage seemed to be doing great in front of the cameras, things weren’t so great at home. Because in 2012, Holmes decided to divorce the Mission: Impossible actor.

    "Kate has filed for divorce and Tom is deeply saddened and is concentrating on his three children. Please allow them their privacy," read a statement issued by Cruise’s reps. Ultimately, Holmes got full custody of Suri once the divorce was finalized.

    Katie Holmes Revealed What Tom Cruise’s Relationship With Their Shared Daughter, Suri, Is Really Like

    In a recent interview, Katie Holmes revealed that the media’s claims about Tom Cruise’s relationship with their daughter Suri are “full of inaccuracies.” The Dawson’s Creek star took a jab at the media for the recently resurfaced claims that Suri “does not know her father anymore” and has “no actual relationship” with him. Other sources claim that Suri “does not go to see his movies and he has no part in her life.”

    An article in the Daily Mail even quoted a source close to Holmes who claimed that “While Katie is taking an active role in helping her daughter with college applications, the teen's father, Mission Impossible star Tom, 60, will not play a role in the process - having been estranged from his daughter since 2013, one year after he and Katie divorced.”

    Related: How Suri Cruise Really Feels About Her Father Tom Cruise Not Being In Her Life Anymore?

    Shortly after this article was published, these new rumors of Tom Cruise and his daughter went viral. But the Dawson’s Creek star quickly shot them down, claiming that they were very inaccurate. Although Holmes didn’t elaborate on what her daughter’s relationship with Cruise is actually like, this is the first time in over a decade that the actress has openly talked about their relationship.

    Fans couldn’t help to notice how odd it is that Holmes defended the Mission: Impossible actor as a father to Suri so shortly after it was rumored that Cruise quit Scientology because it kept him away from his daughter.

    Tom Cruise Has Paid $4.8 Million in Child Support for His and Katie Holmes' Daughter, Suri

    According to the Daily Mail, “Tom Cruise has always maintained his responsibilities when it comes to financial obligations to Katie and Suri.” This includes covering anything that Suri might need, including "medical, dental, insurance, education, college and other extracurricular costs."

    This hasn’t been cheap to the Mission: Impossible actor. In fact, it’s estimated that Cruise has paid over $4.8 million in child support since his divorce from Holmes. According to reports, Cruise has paid Holmes $400,000 a year in child support since 2012, which comes to $33,333.33 a month.

    However, Cruises’s child support payments are about to stop since Suri turning 18 soon. Only time will tell if Suri will choose to have a closer relationship with her father moving forward. For her part, Holmes has admitted that she’ll support whatever decision her daughter makes.
