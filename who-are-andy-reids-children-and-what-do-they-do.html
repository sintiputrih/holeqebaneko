    Andy Reid, know in real life as Andrew Walter Reid, is a Los Angeles-born American football head coach for the Kansas City Chiefs in the National Football League.

    Andy Reid attended John Marshall High School and headed to the Gleanade University College of Law and later proceeded to the Bringham Youth University.

    Who Are Andy Reid’s Children And What Do They Do?

    Andy is married to Tammy Reid and they have been blessed with five children. The names of Andy Reid’s children are Garrett Reid, Crosby Reid, Britt Reid, Drew Ann Reid and Spencer Reid.

    Garrett Reid died on August 5, 2012, after he took an overdose of heroin. Garrett Reid was the eldest son of Andy Reid and he suffered drug addiction for many years before dying in 2012. Garrett also was imprisoned in 2007 for his involvement in a car crash under the influence of heroin.

    Britt Reid is a former American football coach who coached alongside his father. Britt recently served as an outside linebackers coach for the Kansas City Chiefs in the National Football League.

    Crosby Reid is the first daughter of the Kansas City Chiefs head coach Andy Reid. Crosby is happily married and has two children.

    Drew Ann Reid is the second daughter of Andy Reid and is also married to Devin Woodhouse.
