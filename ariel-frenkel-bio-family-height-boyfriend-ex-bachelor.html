    Ariel Frenkel Family

    Ariel Frenkel is one of the two children of Ukrainian-Jewish parents Feliks and Marina Frenkel. Her dad is a Russian-Jewish immigrant.

    Feliks’s grandfather on his father’s side was involved in Schul and Jewish communal life. His maternal grandfather was who Feliks called “a real businessman”, an entrepreneur, and described him as Warren Buffett of his family.

    Feliks’s dad was a graduate of Leningrad Military Academy. He served as an officer in the Soviet army and was also a civil engineer. Feliks’s parents met in the ’40s and married in 1946. His mother was an attorney.

    Feliks had aspirations to become a zoologist and dreams of traveling to the White Sea. His mother desired for him to become a civil engineer like his father. Later, he moved to Siberia in the City of Omsk where he lived for five years where he got his civil engineering degree. After Feliks and his family moved to the US, he took three jobs in Philadelphia.

    Though he was offered an assistant baker job, he started moving garbage cans. Later on in his life, he got introduced to Mercantile Exchange at the existing World Trade Center. His experience as a young boy trading gums came in handy for him. Now, he is defined as a private investor, community leader, and philanthropist.

    He is 67 years of age in 2023. Ariel’s mom Marina is a co-founder of Merchant Pro Express.

    Ariel has a brother named Bobby Frenkel. Bobby is a Licensed Real Estate Salesperson at Compass at Compass starting from 2023. He graduated from Baruch College in 2012.
