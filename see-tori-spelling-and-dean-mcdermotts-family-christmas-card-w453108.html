    It’s the most wonderful time of the year! Tori Spelling and Dean McDermott shared their family’s adorable annual Christmas card with fans on Monday, November 21.

    “The holidays are officially upon us, and this is hands-down our favorite time of the year in the McDermott household,” the former Beverly Hills, 90210 star, 43, wrote on her website, ediTORIal. “We’re so incredibly grateful for another amazing year, and I couldn’t feel more lucky to have such a wonderful husband and such incredible, smart, hilarious little ones (with another on the way!!).”

    The reality show family have made a tradition of sharing their holiday cards with fans in years past.

    “We’re excited to celebrate all the holiday traditions we love so much … making Christmas cookies, DIY’ing up a storm, whipping up our fave holiday foods, decorating the tree, and watching our family’s favorite cult classic holiday movies together,” she continued.

    In the sweet snapshot, Spelling sits front and center with McDermott, 50, on her right and their four children — Liam, 9, Stella, 8, Hattie, 5, and Finn, 4 — gathered around them on a vintage sofa. Spelling shows just the slightest hint of a baby bump underneath her navy blue gown. (She and McDermott confirmed their fifth pregnancy to Us Weekly in early October; McDermott is dad to son Jack, 17, with his ex-wife, Mary Jo Eustace.)

    The reality star told Entertainment Tonight that they have yet to learn the baby-to-be’s gender, and don’t mind the suspense. “We have two of each, so we’re blessed,” she told ET in October. “We did find out with the first two, didn’t find out with the second two — huge, amazing surprises. The kids are old enough now they kind of want to find out, you know. We’ve taken a family vote so at some point we might find out but not yet.”
