    Kylie Jenner is proving that style just gets better with age.

    The Kylie Cosmetics founder celebrated her 25th birthday yesterday with friends and family, capping off her night with an extravagant display of fireworks. She shared photos of her special birthday look on Instagram, too, in which she posed in a nearly sheer, off-the-shoulder gown. The opalescent dress had a body-hugging, skintight silhouette and was completely covered in shimmering beads.

    To accessorize, Jenner opted for dazzling diamond chandelier earrings. She kept her glam elevated and elegant, wearing her hair up in a bun with loose curled strands framing her face. She also sported a subtle smoky cat eye and a rosy lip.

    A source for Entertainment Tonight recently opened up about how the young mogul is faring in regard to her life as a mom of two with longtime boyfriend Travis Scott.

    "Things between Kylie and Travis are going super well. They have really mastered being together and successfully co-parenting," the insider said. "It's really working for them and they are just living life. They're doing fantastic as parents of two."

    Jenner and Scott welcomed their first child, Stormi Webster, in February 2018. Though rumors of a breakup emerged in 2019, the two welcomed their second child, a baby boy whose name they have yet to disclose, in March of this year.

    "Travis is very involved as a father and Kylie loves that," the source added. "He really makes Kylie and his family a top priority, which is a big reason as to why they work as a couple. He is always going above and beyond as a dad and as a partner and Kylie appreciates his dedication to her and their little ones."
