    Wondering what to buy for your mom, aunt, sister, and niece this holiday season in one fell swoop? Lululemon has a section of specials for Black Friday that will appeal to all. You’ll find everything from the butter-soft Align leggings to sleek and practical accessories such as a wool-blend knit beanie and a water-repellent belt bag.

    We scoured the We Made Too Much section for the best leggings, sports bras, sneakers, and more — up to $139 off — to make holiday shopping easier than ever. (Truly, there’s something for everyone!) Keep scrolling for these epic Black Friday specials, and add them to your cart before they sell out.

    Best Lululemon Bottoms for Black Friday

    Lululemon’s Align leggings are a fan favorite, to be sure, likely thanks to its buttery fabric. The brand’s signature Nulu fabric is designed to feel weightless and breathable. It’s bolstered by Lycra to keep the leggings’ shape with continuous wear and to wick sweat away from the body. The style is offered with multiple rises, inseam variations, color options, and discount rates.

    The Wunder Train High-Rise Tights are available, too, at a considerably lower price — up to $79 off. The fast-drying fabric supports serious training, so wearers can even take a boxing class without worrying about patches of sweat becoming visible as they move.

    For those in warmer temperatures, there are several shorts options to snatch up, including the License to Train High-Rise Short. It offers a relaxed fit for the full freedom to move through burpees and jump rope sessions. The form-fitting Align High-Rise Shorts come in several colors and inseams for up to 47 percent off.

    Best Lululemon Tops for Black Friday

    Build a look with a coordinated bra or top. We love the Align V-Neck Bra to go with a pair of leggings or shorts, and in colder temperatures, we’d round it out with the Fleece + Ripstop Hiking Pullover for $99.

    The Hold Tight Tank Top is another staple, available in seven versatile neutrals. It’s designed for everyday wear with softness and stretch that make it feel like a second skin (minus the ribbed texture). The longer length, however, is perhaps an acquired taste. For something cropped, on the other hand, there’s the Classic-Fit Cotton-Blend Tank Top. It’s a bit looser, too, for a breezy feel.

    Other Lululemon Favorites for Black Friday

    If you’re feeling all too generous, go the extra mile with any of the below favorites, like the Wunder Puff Cropped Vest — the best deal on this list at up to $139 off — or the classic License to Train hat, which is made for training in the open air with its water-repellent fabric and low-profile sweatband.

    And still, there’s no shame in a little reward to yourself for checking so many gifts off your list early and saving money on top of it. The Daily Stride Comfort Crew Sock (5-Pack) is an easy addition to help you brighten up your look and feel supported, too, with arch support and extra toe cushioning. There’s also the Strongfeel Training Shoes, which are half-price right now, and we have a feeling you’ll be so taken with their lightweight feel that you won’t want to take them off.
