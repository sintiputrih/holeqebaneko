    Selena Gomez may not have been awarded a Golden Globe last night, but she did take home the most important gift of all: her love.

    The singer and actor, who was nominated for Best Female Actor for her role in the Hulu comedy-mystery series Only Murders in the Building, shared several behind-the-scenes photos from the award show on her Instagram Story. In one of them, she was seen passionately kissing boyfriend Benny Blanco.

    The snap seemed to have been taken in a private room—filled with packages—while both Gomez and Blanco were still in their red-carpet looks. In the photo, Gomez sits on Blanco’s lap and kisses him while sweetly embracing his neck with one hand. He, meanwhile, holds her waist.

    “I won,” the star captioned the image of the romantic moment.

    Gomez wore a ruby-red silk gown custom-made by Giorgio Armani Privé to attend the 81st Golden Globes at the Beverly Hilton hotel in Los Angeles. In the photo, she layered on a black jacket, which she wore draped below her shoulders.

    courtesy of Selena Gomez via Instagram

    Gomez and Blanco were first seen together in December, but have been dating for several months now. In recent weeks, they’ve begun showing off their romance more and more, often sharing snaps of one another on their social media channels. On December 30, for example, Gomez shared photos of Blanco hugging her from behind while kissing her on the cheek.

    “He is my absolute everything in my heart,” she wrote on a fan page last month, after a picture of them together was shared. She also responded to fan comments, saying she is at her “happiest” and Blanco is “the best thing that's ever happened to me. The end.”
