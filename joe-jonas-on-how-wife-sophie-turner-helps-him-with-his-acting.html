    Joe Jonas is breaking into Hollywood, and his talented wife is helping him all the way through.

    The Jonas Brothers singer is set to make his feature debut with his new wartime film, Devotion, which premieres this November 23 and tells the story of how fighter pilots Jesse Brown and Tom Hudner became the U.S. Navy's most celebrated wingmen during the Korean War.

    In a new interview with Entertainment Tonight, the singer shared exactly how wife Sophie Turner has been helping him be a better actor.

    "She tells me to do it again, and do it again and do it again," Jonas joked. "Luckily, just watching her do her thing is always inspiring."

    Turner entered the spotlight with her role in Game of Thrones and has since appeared in X-Men: Dark Phoenix, Josie, Heavy, and Do Revenge, among other film and TV projects.

    "I mean, I feel so grateful that I get to have somebody like her behind the camera when it's time to put myself on tape," Jonas said of having the mother of his two children as his coach.
