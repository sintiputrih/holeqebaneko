    Spider-Man: Miles Morales has a few hidden Trophies around the city. You can get a trophy for jumping onto a derelict boat in Chinatown or finding a statue of the legendary father of the Marvel universe, Stan Lee. But there’s also a hidden trophy for something much more personal for Miles. You’ll get the Never Give Up trophy for visiting Jefferson Davis — Miles’ father — at his gravesite in Harlem.

    If you earned the similar Trophy in Marvel’s Spider-Man — given out for visiting Uncle Ben’s grave — you should know where you’re going. If this is your first trip to the Manhattan cemetery then open your map. Scan to the top of Harlem and look on the left side. There’s a square patch where there are no buildings. Set yourself a waypoint and head there.

    Once you’re in the cemetery you want to head farther north, beyond the path and toward the back fence (check out our positioning on the map above).

    When you’re nearby the waypoint, start tapping R3 to scan. Look around for a small, orange object nearby. When you spot it, head over and interact. This is the headstone of Jefferson Davis. You’ll get a short, reflective scene with Miles as well as the Never Give Up trophy.
