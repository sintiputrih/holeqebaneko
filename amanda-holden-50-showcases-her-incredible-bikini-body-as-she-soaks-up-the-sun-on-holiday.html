    16 Feb 2024, 00:00

    AMANDA Holden looks absolutely incredible as she soaks up the sun poolside on a family holiday. 

    The 50-year-old Britain’s Got Talent judge was age-defying as she posed for a sultry bikini pic on the edge of her infinity pool. 

    Wearing a tiny string bikini, Amanda arched her back and ran her fingers through her hair as she lay down with sunglasses on. 

    Uploading the pic to Instagram, she wrote alongside the pic: “Just #chillin”. 

    Amanda is currently celebrating some much-needed downtime with a family getaway to a hot and exotic location. 

    Staying in a luxury villa, she has been sharing plenty of snaps to keep us jealous – either with her time in the ocean, with her killer figure, or both. 

    When not posing by the pool during a tanning session, the Heart Radio presenter has also shared some adorable photos with her family. 

    One stunning selfie showed Amanda with her hair in loose curls cuddling up to her husband Chris Hughes, while another she beamed proudly next to her daughters – Alexa, 15, and nine-year-old Hollie.

    While the destination remains unknown, it’s certainly somewhere to be envious of, with the family spending the day taking a dip in the sea with clear blue skies as far as the eye can see. 

    Mountains can also be seen in the background of the most recent picture. 

    Jealous? Us? Never.
