    The use of cannabis has been around for centuries, and it is no surprise that celebrities partake in smoking or ingesting marijuana and other drugs. For many stars, there’s a certain allure associated with the lifestyle that comes with being high on weed. They often see it as a way to escape their overwhelming celebrity responsibilities — like interviews, photoshoots, performances, and so forth.

    Celebrity culture can also be quite competitive when it comes to who’s “cooler” between different stars. Smoking weed might be seen as an act of rebellion and trying something new among fellow actors or musicians. Many people look up to celebrities — if they’re doing it, others will follow suit in order to fit in.

    Though marijuana use carries some risks and is illegal in many places, celebrities often have access to high-quality strains with incredibly low THC levels. This means that they can get the desired effects without worrying about getting too stoned or having any unwanted side effects. Even so, it’s important to be conscious of these potential risks and make sure that you don’t overdo it.

    Finally, marijuana can also provide a sense of relaxation for those who are constantly under stress. Whether it’s related to their celebrity life or personal troubles, cannabis can help stars find some peace of mind — even if only for a few hours at a time. With all this in mind, it’s no wonder why celebrities like smoking marijuana and getting high on weed.

    9 Celebrities You Didn’t Know that Smoke Marijuana

    The use of marijuana and weed by celebrities has been a topic of debate for many years. It is widely accepted that it is a recreational drug, but there are also those who see it as a natural medicine or pain reliever. Despite this fact, many well-known celebrities still continue to smoke in public today — here are some of the most notable ones.

    Miley Cyrus

    Miley Cyrus is one of the most well-known celebrities who openly admits to smoking marijuana. She has become a poster child for cannabis culture, often seen wearing clothing and accessories that contain references to her love for the substance. Miley’s music also often contains lyrics about weed, including her hit single “We Can’t Stop” which contains a reference to “blazing on the barbeque.”

    Jennifer Lawrence

    Jennifer Lawrence is another high-profile celebrity who enjoys smoking marijuana. She has been open about her habit in interviews, saying it helps her relax and cope with stress. The Hunger Games star also famously smoked weed during an appearance on The Tonight Show With Jay Leno back in 2013, although she later admitted that it wasn’t actually marijuana but oregano.

    Seth Rogen

    Seth Rogen is one of Hollywood’s most prolific stoners, having seemingly made a career out of playing characters who are passionate about cannabis culture. It should come as no surprise then that Rogen himself is openly fond of smoking marijuana, often tweeting and speaking openly about his love for the substance.

    Paris Hilton

    Paris Hilton is one of the most unlikely celebrity smokers, but it seems she enjoys indulging on occasion. The heiress has been seen smoking cannabis at parties and in public, often posting photos or videos online to her millions of fans.

    Kevin Smith

    Kevin Smith is another well-known celebrity who loves to smoke weed. The Clerks director/actor/writer regularly posts photos and videos of himself smoking to his social media accounts, often pairing it with some hilarious captions.

    Pete Davidson

    Pete Davidson is a more recent addition to the list of famous celebrity smokers. He’s been vocal about his love for marijuana in interviews and on social media, even rapping about it on Saturday Night Live.

    Snoop Dogg

    Snoop Dogg needs no introduction when it comes to the world of weed. He’s been an avid smoker for years and his love for marijuana culture has even spilled over into his music career with countless references throughout his songs. He’s also a big advocate for legalizing cannabis, setting up companies that offer products related to marijuana consumption.

    Jay Z

    Jay Z might not be as open about his habit as other celebrities, but he has been known to indulge from time to time. The rapper/entrepreneur was caught on tape smoking weed during an impromptu jam session back in 2008 and there have been rumors that he partakes from time to time when away from the public eye.

    Rihanna

    Rihanna is another major celebrity who enjoys getting high every once in a while. She’s been spotted at various events and parties smoking weed, with photos often appearing online shortly after.

    Brad Pitt

    Last but not least we have Brad Pitt. He might be one of the more low-key celebrity smokers on this list, but there have been reports that he enjoys partaking in the occasional puff or two when away from prying eyes. It seems his love for marijuana goes back to his early days in Hollywood, as he was reportedly caught by police in 1988 for possession of cannabis.

    Celebrities Enjoying the High Life

    All these celebrities and many others prove that enjoying a little marijuana every now and then doesn’t make you any less successful or famous – it just makes you human! So don’t be afraid to indulge in some cannabis culture every once in a while. Just make sure you do it responsibly and legally.
