    Lilia Buckingham (born April 9, 2003) is an American Actress, Producer, Competitive Dancer, and Social Media Star from Los Angeles, California. She is popular for being one of the dancers at the MNR Dance Factory.

    As a performer, Lilia won the Regional Junior Miss Thunderstruck Dance Icon 2015 as well as the Regional Junior Rainbow Rising Starz DOY 2013.

    In the starting days of her career, the actress appeared in various big shows such as “Good Morning America”, “Day & Night”, and “Modern Family”. Her presence and activeness on social media also trigger her fame on the internet.
