    Kimberly Armstrong Missing

    Kimberly Armstrong has been reported missing after she left for Walmart in Lowville from a doctor’s appointment in Lyon Falls. No one has heard from her since 3-4 p.m. on Friday afternoon. She was driving a 2008 grayish-tan Envoy with a rack on top for canoes. Kim is described as a solid and stable person, last seen wearing a yellow/gold sweater, with blonde hair, blue eyes, and a height of 5 foot 7 inches. She is currently in the process of writing a children’s book and is close to retiring as a Postmaster. Kim loves her husband and being a grandmother (“Lolli”) to her grandkids.
