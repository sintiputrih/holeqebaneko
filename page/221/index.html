How do I remove ivy from my house?

Steps: Gently pull ivy from house. When necessary, cut stubborn ivy from house with hand pruner. Scrape siding to remove dead branches and dried leaves. Sand the siding with orbital sander to remove stubborn ivy stains and residue. Cut the ivy stems close to ground with loppers. What kills ivy permanently?
