What causes white spots on bougainvillea leaves

02 Jun 2024, 00:00

How do you treat bougainvillea leaf spots? Remove badly infected leaves. Collect and dispose of fallen leaves. Provide the plant with excellent air circulation and avoid wetting the foliage when watering. It is generally not necessary to put bougainvillea plants on a regular spray schedule with fungicides for this fairly common problem. Why does my bougainvillea have white spots? The white spots can be damage from chemical sprays, watering issues (don’t keep the soil too moist) or the beginning of a fungal or bacterial disease.
