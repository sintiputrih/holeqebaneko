2nm in 2025, 1.4nm in 2027

14 May 2024, 00:00

Samsung Foundry revealed its latest process technology roadmap today at its annual Samsung Foundry Forum (SFF) 2023. The company's SF2 (2 nm-class) production node is on track for 2025, whereas its successor SF1.4 (1.4 nm-class) is expected to be available in 2027. Meanwhile, the company published some of the characteristics it expects from its SF2 manufacturing process. Samsung's SF2 process technology, which will be available to the company's clients in 2025, will offer a 25% higher power efficiency (at the same clocks and complexity), a 12% increase in performance (at the same power and complexity), and a 5% decrease in area when compared to SF3, the company's 2nm Generation 3 nm-class node introduced earlier this year.
