    Torrie Wilson is ready and willing to be a member of Judgment Day, or a victim of Rhea Ripley.

    WWE Hall of Famer Torrie Wilson recently spoke with Bill Apter of Sportskeeda. When asked who she thinks is the top female performer in wrestling right now, Wilson admitted that she’s obsessed with Rhea Ripley.

    “Yeah, there’s a lot of really good ones, but the one I’m obsessed with right now [is] Rhea Ripley; who isn’t? She is such a badass,” Torrie Wilson said. “I mean, they’re all incredible. And I’ve gone definitely gone through periods where I’ve been super obsessed with Charlotte and then super obsessed with Sasha Banks. But I mean, they’re all up there, right? But Rhea Ripley right now is just shot killing it.”

    When Apter asked Wilson to cut a promo on Ripley for a potential fantasy match-up, things took a hilarious turn.

    “I’m gonna step into that ring, and I’m going to be ready to kick your ass,” Torrie Wilson began. “You know what? It’s gonna take one move. One little bitch slap to my face, and I’ll be down. I know you got me beat, but you know what? I’m so obsessed with you, I’ll let you pin me. I’ll let you kick my ass for free. Okay, that’s my promo on you. That’s a horrible promo, but I love her so much.”

    When asked if she wanted to join The Judgment Day, Wilson admitted that she did, although she’d had to turn heel in the process.

    “Yeah, I do kind of love them,” Torrie Wilson admitted. “I’d have to turn heel, but sometimes you got to do that.”
