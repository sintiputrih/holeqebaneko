    Is Bluey From Blue’s Clues Boy Or A Girl?

    Bluey from Blue’s Clues is a female puppy who communicates to her owners Steve, Joe, and Josh. She attends a school that makes her famous between both kids and the family. 

    Her owners can partially understand with her expression and tone. She is brilliant, friendly, energetic, and playful. Like any other puppy, she sometimes gets angry or sad. 

    However, what makes her different is she loves to read, draw pictures, or listen to music. She is described as the smartest of all the puppies. Blue is a sky blue dog with darker blue spots and a dark blue nose. 

    Blue also has her few dedicated fandom pages on the internet. In addition, she is also the only character to appear in a credit sentence during the show’s run from season 1 to 6.

    Traci Paige Johnson gives voice to the beloved character Bluey. She is also one of the three creators of the series. Johnson has given voice for both the original and the reboot.
