    Kevin Hart is one of the biggest stars in Hollywood and one of the most successful standup comedians of all time. Whether it’s from his starring roles in Central Intelligence, Ride Along, Get Hard, or Jumanji, Hart has become a household name around the world for his acting ability alongside his comedy quips.

    Recommended Videos

    From the beginning of his career, Hart has used his height as a comedic device as one of the shorter people amongst his peers and this has continued through into his film career. For fans, we see him on the big screen standing beside giants like Dwayne “The Rock” Johnson, but the question remains:

    How tall is Kevin Hart?

    Kevin Hart stands somewhere between 5’2” and 5’4”. This might not be such as a surprise given the emphasis that Hart puts on this fact within his comedy. However, as a lead male, traditionally actors are much taller. Despite his shortcomings (no pun intended), Hart has heaped praise on his height stating that nothing special comes from being tall and that personality “is the biggest and best thing you could have.”

    The lack of height hasn’t stopped Hart from propelling himself into stardom with his charismatic personality and impressive acting chops. Hart is the perfect example of embracing who you are, remaining true to yourself, and putting in maximum effort in succeeding within your field.

    If you’re wanting to see a Hart’s height on full display, you can catch him alongside goliath The Rock in these classic films.
