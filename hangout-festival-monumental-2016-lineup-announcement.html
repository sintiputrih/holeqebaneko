    If it seems a little early for music festivals to begin unveiling their 2016 lineups, that’s probably because it is – but apparently Hangout Beach, Music and Arts Festival didn’t get that memo. Even though the Gulf Shores, Alabama event doesn’t take place until May, its organizers have already revealed a lineup that’s sure to give other festivals a run for their money.

    Calvin Harris, Flume and The Chainsmokers make up the top tier of EDM headliners on the roster – a list which also includes Run The Jewels, 3LAU, Bro Safari, Snails and Alison Wonderland. Pop sensations such as The Weeknd, Florence & The Machine, Ellie Goulding and Panic At The Disco round out the event’s already formidable draw, making Hangout a massive not to be missed.

    Hangout Beach, Music and Arts festival’s organizers will not be sitting on tickets for long either, as they go on sale tomorrow at 10:00 AM CST.
