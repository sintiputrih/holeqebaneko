    Death

    Actor Heath Ledger was found still in his bed in his loft at Broome Street in the SoHo neighborhood of Manhattan, New York, United States.

    He was found by his massage therapist Diana Wolizin and housekeeper, Teresa Solomon, on 22 January 2008.

    Upon finding Ledger still, Wolizin dialed 911, and at the urging of the 911 operator, she delivered CPR, which was unsuccessful in reviving him.

    Ledger was pronounced dead at 3:36 p.m., and his body was removed from the penthouse apartment.

    He was just 28 at the time of his death.
