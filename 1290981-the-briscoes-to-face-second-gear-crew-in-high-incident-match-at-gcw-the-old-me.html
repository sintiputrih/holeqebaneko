    ECW made the High Incident Match famous at an event named after the match in 1996. The wrestling world has seen other scaffold matches since, but the High Incident is a clear callback to ECW.

    At GCW The Old Me, Minoru Suzuki will face 2 Cold Scorpio and Bandido will face Joey Janela, among other noteworthy bouts.

    • The updated card is as follows:
    • High Incident Match: The Briscoes vs. Second Gear Crew
    • 2 Cold Scorpio vs. Minoru Suzuki
    • Bandido vs. Joey Janela
    • John Wayne Murdoch vs. Hoodfoot
    • Alex Shelley vs. Nick Wayne
    • Chris Dickinson vs. ACH
    • GCW Tag Team Championship: BUSSY (Allie Katch and EFFY) vs. Gringo Loco and ASF
    • AJ Gray vs. Alex Colon
