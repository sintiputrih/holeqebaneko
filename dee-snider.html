    Moving on to his career, he started his career by joining Twisted Sister in early 1976 and became the band’s sole songwriter.     He wrote The Magic of Christmas Day in 1998 which Celine Dion recorded for her album titled These Are Special Times. Later that year, he also wrote and was seen appearing in the horror film titled Strangeland.

    He later returned to radio in June 2006 with Fangoria Radio on Sirius Satellite Radio. Also, he hosted VH1’s Aftermath concert in 2008 in honor of the victims and survivors of the 2003 Station nightclub fire. He with his family debuted on the television show titled Growing Up Twisted on July 27, 2010.
