    Adrienne began working as a voice-over and commercial artist after finishing her MA in Theatre. Two years later, she was cast as “Angel Unaware” in Daniel Beaty’s 2011 world premiere musical, Tearing Down The Walls, for which she received an Audelco nomination for Best Actress.
