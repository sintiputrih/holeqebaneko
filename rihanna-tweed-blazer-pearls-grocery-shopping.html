    Rihanna makes everything a fashion moment, even a routine shopping trip.

    The pop star and beauty mogul was photographed at a Whole Foods in New York City wearing a tweed blazer with shoulder pads and gold hardware, a lacy black bra, and loose acid-washed jeans. She was also seen sporting a pair of white Adidas sneakers and a green Yankees cap.

    The newly crowned billionaire accessorized her outfit with strings of Vivienne Westwood pearls wrapped around her neck, a longer pendant necklace, a gold-and-black chain belt with a number-five pendant, and a large gold ring. She also rocked a bright red lip (assumedly courtesy of Fenty Beauty).

    TheBigSHotApp/Shutterstock//Shutterstock

    The casual-glam outfit is the latest look Rihanna has been spotted wearing while out on the town in New York City. Last week, the superstar wore a Harley-Davidson scarf top, distressed Balenciaga jeans, and another bold red lip for a night out on the town. She has also worn multiple iconic looks on date nights with her boyfriend, A$AP Rocky.

    The entrepreneur currently added a new product to her Fenty empire with the release of Fenty Eau de Parfum. Fenty's first fragrance immediately sold out on release, and Rihanna celebrated in style, posting a picture of herself celebrating with caviar in bed.

    The perfume was designed intentionally down to the bottle, which was made to fit perfectly in Rihanna's palm. She said, "I wanted a rich color that really represented all genders from across all walks of life. Brown is my favorite color. Brown is who I am. Brown is what I come from. If I'm going to make a fragrance that represents me, even the body language of the bottle needs to marry that."
