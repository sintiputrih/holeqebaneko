    Kenny Loggins Biography

    Kenny Loggins is an American Musician, Singer and Songwriter, who was born and brought up in Everett, Washington, United States of America. He is widely known for his cover of a country version of “Danger Zone” as a duet with the character of Cherlene, which earned him a nomination of a Grammy Award for Best Male Pop Vocal Performance. He is also known for recording and co-writing “Footloose”, which also earned him five nominations of Academy Awards, Golden Globe Awards, two Grammy Awards and a Tony Awards.
