    Nau (Nauzet Gutiérrez Hernández) – Professional Life

    Early career

    In 2013, at the age of 11 years old, his seven-year-old brother started singing at a family celebration climbing on the table. Their family event was also attended by popular composer and singer Iván Troyano. Impressed by their talent, he went to the family, proposing to collaborate with the boy.

    Likewise, their official YouTube channel ‘Adexe & Nau‘ has gained more than 12.6 million subscribers to date.

    Major work

    Talking about Nauzet Gutiérrez Hernández’s professional life, he made his debut along with his brother as a duo in June 2015. They debuted with a cover of Nicky Jam’s “Si Tu No Estas.” The Spanish brothers Nau & Adexe has delivered sunny reggaeton-tinged Latin pop similar to other Joey Montana, CNCO, and Naim & Marcos.

    Since then, they have also made cover song videos for Felipe Santos, Prince Royce, and Justin Bieber. On February 10, 2017, they signed a contract of a record deal with Sony Music Latin. Both Spanish and American media reported about their contract.

    However, they started as solo performers, uploading cover songs online before joining with a cover of Nicky Jam’s “Si Tu No Estas.” They have produced more covers, one of which was their popular take on Chino & Nacho’s collaboration with Daddy Yankee, “Andas en Mi Cabeza.” On May 5, 2017, they make their debut full-length song, Tu & Yo with Sony Music.

    ‘Tú y Yo’ is of 12 songs: the title track, ‘Podemos Ser Felices’, ‘Soñando Contigo’, ‘En Otro Mundo Diferente’, ‘Te Estoy Haciendo Enloquecer’, ‘Yo Quiero Vivir’, ‘Sólo Amigos’, ‘Es Para Mi Señorita’, ‘Navegando’, ‘Grita’, ‘Nunca Llorarás’, and ‘Es Amor’. In the same year, he along with his brother even contributed to the soundtrack of the fantasy action film ‘Jumanji: Welcome to the Jungle’.

    Likewise, several of their most popular covers are of songs by Felipe Santos, Prince Royce, and Justin Bieber. They have gained popularity through their brilliant covers on YouTube as well as gained many fans on the channel.
