    Who is Lea Seydoux?

    Lea Seydoux’s full name is Lea Hélène Seydoux-Fornier de Clausonne. She was born in Passy, Paris France.

    She is a famous French actress and model who has worked in both French and American movies.

    She has worked in many movies and won many awards and nominations for her role.

    Early Life of Lea Seydoux

    Lea Seydoux was born and grew up in Saint-Germain-des-Prés and was raised in a strict Protestant family even though she doesn’t believe in God.

    When she was young, she wanted to be an opera artist. She learned music at the famous ‘Conservatoire de Paris.’ Because of her shy nature, she decided not to go to school.

    At the age of 18, she wanted to be an actress. She went to Les Enfants Terribles, a French drama school, and the Actors Studio in New York to learn acting and fulfill her dream.
