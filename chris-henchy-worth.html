    American writer and producer Chris Henchy has an estimated net worth of $15 million. He is best recognized as the producer of Eastbound & Down, which air HBO. He also co-produced the television series Entourage, as well as The Campaign. Henchy is also one of the founders and creators of the website known as Funny or Die. As a writer, Henchy has worked on shows such as I’m With Her, Spin City, The Other Guys, The Campaign and Land of the Lost. 

    Chris Henchy was born Christopher Thomas “Chris” Henchy on March 23, 1964 in New York City. Since 2001, he has been married to Brooke Shields. In July 2000, the couple got engaged and by April 2001, the two got married. The couple has two children together named Rowan Frances (b. 2003), and Grier Hammond (b. 2006).
